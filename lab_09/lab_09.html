Airfryer Fish Tacos

Cooking Instructions

Step 1:

  • Using a paper towel, gently dry off the fish to remove any watery substances.
  • Stir together cilantro, lime juice, bread crumbs, garlic, and 1/2 teaspoon of the salt in a medium bowl; then mix.

Step 2:

  • Take 1 fish fillet and place it into the combined spices and flip to cover both sides with seasoning.
  • Spray the base of the airfryer with cooking oil
  • Place the fillet into the airfryer
  • Repeat with all fillets
  • Close the airfryer and cook at 390 degrees for 10 minutes. (flip fillets at 5 minutes)

Step 3:

  • Remove the fillets and slice into smaller pieces

Step 4:

  • Heat tacos if desired
  • Place fillet bits into the taco shell
  • Add any additional toppings or sauces if desired

ENJOY!
