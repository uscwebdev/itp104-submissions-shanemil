Recipe of the Day

Air Fryer Chicken Breast Meal Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon/Lemon Juice
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Garlic
  • Scallions
  • Paprika
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Optional: Cauliflower Rice & Brussel Sprouts
Chicken Instructions
  1. Prepare:Place chicken in zip lock bag and add small amount of oil, spices, and then gently rub after sealing bag.
  2. Air Fry:Place marinated chicken and brussel sprouts in Airfryer and bake at 400 degrees for 7 minutes. (flip at 3:30)
  3. Microwave:Keep cauliflower rice in the bag and place into the microwave until directed. (Kirkland Cauliflower Rice is 6-7 minutes)
