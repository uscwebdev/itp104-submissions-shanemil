Topic

My website topic will be about recipes. More specifically, I will be including easy and healthy meals to prepare. This website will be broken down into Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Desserts. Submenus will be the individual recipes to follow.

Audience

The intended audience will be college students looking to make easy meals with air fryers or stovetop meals ready in just a few minutes.

Design & Style

The design will be broken down as follows. A picture of the item at the top with the heading, then the recipe with an image, followed by more thorough instructions. Color designs will vary with each dish.

Color Pallet

  • Breakfast: Yellow, Secondary: lighter shades of orange.
  • Lunch: Blue, Secondary: lighter shades of blue and bluish-green
  • Dinner: Mossy Green, Secondary: lighter shades of green still working with the text colors.
  • Desserts: Pink, Secondary; lighter pink and purple
